AI for People & Nature

The second gathering to advance AI for Climate Adaptation at NYC Climate Week

Here’s the concept:

We ask

AI-enabled adaptation solutions are already working.

So what would it take to move even a tiny fraction of the trillions of dollars flowing into AI to the billions of people facing the worst of climate change?1

So at Climate Week…

We bring together the 3 audiences who all know parts of the answer:

  • Nonprofits with solutions
  • Tech Companies with infrastructure
  • Funders from philanthropy and impact investing

How the event will flow:

A tight 2 hours where every segment builds towards answers.
